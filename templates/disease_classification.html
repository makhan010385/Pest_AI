Common Plant Diseases
Leaf Blight

Brown or black spots on leaves, often with yellow halos. Common in humid conditions.

Powdery Mildew

White, powdery coating on leaves and stems. Thrives in warm, dry conditions.

Rust Disease

Orange or rust-colored spots on leaves. Spreads rapidly in moist conditions.
