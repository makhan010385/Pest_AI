Common Agricultural Weeds
Dandelion

Perennial weed with yellow flowers and deeply toothed leaves. Deep taproot makes removal challenging.

Broadleaf
Crabgrass

Annual grass weed that spreads rapidly by seeds. Low-growing with finger-like seed heads.

Grass
Clover

Nitrogen-fixing legume with three-leaflet leaves. Can be beneficial but competitive in crops.

Integrated Weed Management Strategies
Prevention

Clean seeds, equipment sanitation, quarantine

Cultural

Crop rotation, cover crops, competitive planting

Mechanical

Tillage, mowing, hand weeding, mulching

Biological

Natural enemies, allelopathy, competition

Seasonal Weed Management Calendar
Winter
Plan strategies, prepare equipment, apply pre-emergent herbicides
Spring
Early cultivation, spot treatments, monitor emergence
Summer
Post-emergent control, cultivation, hand weeding
Fall
Prevent seed set, cover crops, field cleanup
